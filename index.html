UGA HEROs

HEROs is an organization here at the University of Georgia that works with many kids infected or affected by HIV/AIDS. It provides care and raises money for children to help them through the challenges that they face.

Many kids in the program have experienced feelings of sadness, confusion, anger, or even a loss of hope. Our job in HEROs is to change that outlook and give them a new found hope. We raise money for programs that help these children with HIV/AIDS and bring awareness to the community about the struggle these children face in their everyday lives.

To get involved on campus it is very easy. You can go online to ugaheros.org and join a team. All you have to do is fill out an application and request to join a team. Once on a team, you go to meetings once a week and can attend as many fundraisers as you want. It's an easy way to get involved and stand up for a good cause. It isn't time consuming, you meet a ton of new people, and you're representing children that need our help. Join UGA HEROs today!
